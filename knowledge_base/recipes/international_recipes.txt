--- RECIPE: Vegetable Fried Rice ---
Category: Vegan, Rice, Asian, Quick
Cuisine: Chinese, Indo-Chinese
Prep Time: 10 minutes
Cook Time: 15 minutes
Total Time: 25 minutes
Servings: 4
Difficulty: Easy
Dietary: Vegan (without eggs), Vegetarian

Ingredients:
- 3 cups cooked rice (day-old preferred)
- 2 eggs (optional, omit for vegan)
- 1 cup mixed vegetables (peas, carrots, corn, beans)
- 1 onion, finely chopped
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 tablespoons soy sauce
- 1 tablespoon sesame oil
- 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
- 1/2 teaspoon white pepper
- Spring onions for garnish
- Salt to taste

Instructions:
1. Heat oil in a wok or large pan on high heat.
2. If using eggs, scramble them first and set aside.
3. Add garlic and onion, stir-fry for 1 minute.
4. Add mixed vegetables and stir-fry on high heat for 2-3 minutes.
5. Add rice and break any clumps. Toss on high heat.
6. Add soy sauce, sesame oil, and white pepper.
7. Toss everything together for 2-3 minutes.
8. Add eggs back if using. Mix well.
9. Garnish with spring onions.

Nutrition (per serving): Calories: 320 | Protein: 9g | Carbs: 54g | Fat: 8g
Tips: High heat is essential for fried rice. Day-old cold rice works best as it doesn't stick together.

--- RECIPE: Pasta Arrabbiata ---
Category: Vegan, Pasta, Italian
Cuisine: Italian
Prep Time: 10 minutes
Cook Time: 20 minutes
Total Time: 30 minutes
Servings: 4
Difficulty: Easy
Dietary: Vegan, Vegetarian

Ingredients:
- 400g pasta (penne or rigatoni)
- 4 tomatoes, chopped (or 400g canned tomatoes)
- 6 cloves garlic, minced
- 3 tablespoons olive oil
- 2-4 dried red chilies or 1 teaspoon red pepper flakes
- Fresh basil leaves
- Salt and pepper
- Parmesan cheese for serving (optional, skip for vegan)

Instructions:
1. Cook pasta in salted boiling water until al dente. Reserve 1 cup pasta water.
2. Heat olive oil in a large pan. Add garlic and chilies.
3. Cook until garlic is golden (don't burn it).
4. Add tomatoes, salt, and pepper. Cook 15 minutes until sauce thickens.
5. Add cooked pasta to sauce. Toss to coat.
6. Add pasta water if needed for consistency.
7. Garnish with fresh basil.

Nutrition (per serving): Calories: 380 | Protein: 12g | Carbs: 68g | Fat: 8g | Fiber: 4g
Tips: Reserve pasta water - the starch helps bind sauce to pasta. Authentic arrabbiata is quite spicy.

--- RECIPE: Shakshuka ---
Category: Vegetarian, Eggs, Middle Eastern, Breakfast
Cuisine: Middle Eastern, North African
Prep Time: 10 minutes
Cook Time: 20 minutes
Total Time: 30 minutes
Servings: 2-3
Difficulty: Easy
Dietary: Vegetarian, Gluten-Free

Ingredients:
- 4 eggs
- 3 tomatoes, chopped (or 400g canned)
- 1 red bell pepper, chopped
- 1 onion, chopped
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 1 teaspoon cumin
- 1 teaspoon paprika
- 1/2 teaspoon turmeric
- 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
- Salt and pepper
- Fresh parsley or coriander
- Feta cheese (optional)

Instructions:
1. Heat olive oil in a large skillet. Add onion and pepper.
2. Cook until softened (5-7 minutes). Add garlic.
3. Add all spices and cook 1 minute.
4. Add tomatoes and simmer 10 minutes until sauce thickens.
5. Make 4 wells in the sauce. Crack eggs into each well.
6. Cover and cook until whites are set but yolks are still runny (5-7 minutes).
7. Garnish with parsley and feta if using.
8. Serve with crusty bread or pita.

Nutrition (per serving): Calories: 280 | Protein: 16g | Carbs: 18g | Fat: 18g
Tips: Don't overcook eggs - the runny yolk creates a sauce. Add feta for creaminess.

--- RECIPE: Lentil Soup ---
Category: Vegan, Soup, Mediterranean
Cuisine: Middle Eastern, Mediterranean
Prep Time: 15 minutes
Cook Time: 30 minutes
Total Time: 45 minutes
Servings: 6
Difficulty: Easy
Dietary: Vegan, Vegetarian, Gluten-Free, High-Protein, Diabetic-Friendly

Ingredients:
- 1.5 cups red lentils, rinsed
- 1 onion, chopped
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 tomatoes, chopped
- 2 carrots, diced
- 1 celery stalk, chopped
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 1 teaspoon cumin
- 1 teaspoon turmeric
- 1/2 teaspoon paprika
- 5 cups vegetable broth or water
- Juice of 1 lemon
- Salt and pepper
- Fresh parsley

Instructions:
1. Heat olive oil in a large pot. Sauté onion until soft.
2. Add garlic, carrots, and celery. Cook 5 minutes.
3. Add tomatoes and spices. Cook 2 minutes.
4. Add lentils and broth.
5. Bring to boil, then simmer 25 minutes until lentils are soft.
6. Blend partially for creamy texture (optional).
7. Add lemon juice, adjust seasoning.
8. Serve with bread.

Nutrition (per serving): Calories: 210 | Protein: 13g | Carbs: 33g | Fat: 4g | Fiber: 10g
Tips: Add a squeeze of lemon at the end - it brightens the whole soup. Red lentils cook faster than other varieties.

--- RECIPE: Avocado Toast with Eggs ---
Category: Breakfast, Quick, Western
Cuisine: Contemporary
Prep Time: 5 minutes
Cook Time: 5 minutes
Total Time: 10 minutes
Servings: 2
Difficulty: Easy
Dietary: Vegetarian, High-Protein

Ingredients:
- 2 ripe avocados
- 4 slices whole grain bread
- 4 eggs (poached or fried)
- 1 lemon, juiced
- Salt and pepper
- Red pepper flakes
- Cherry tomatoes, halved
- Microgreens or arugula (optional)

Instructions:
1. Toast bread until golden.
2. Mash avocados with lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
3. Spread avocado on toast.
4. Top with poached or fried eggs.
5. Season with red pepper flakes.
6. Garnish with cherry tomatoes and greens.

Nutrition (per serving): Calories: 420 | Protein: 18g | Carbs: 32g | Fat: 26g | Fiber: 8g
Tips: Avocados should be ripe but firm. Add everything bagel seasoning for extra flavor.

--- RECIPE: Chickpea Stir-Fry ---
Category: Vegan, High-Protein, Quick
Cuisine: Mediterranean, Indian fusion
Prep Time: 5 minutes
Cook Time: 15 minutes
Total Time: 20 minutes
Servings: 3
Difficulty: Easy
Dietary: Vegan, Vegetarian, Gluten-Free, High-Protein

Ingredients:
- 2 cans (400g each) chickpeas, drained
- 1 onion, sliced
- 2 bell peppers, sliced
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 1 teaspoon cumin
- 1 teaspoon paprika
- 1/2 teaspoon turmeric
- Lemon juice
- Fresh parsley

Instructions:
1. Heat oil in pan. Add onions and cook 3 minutes.
2. Add garlic and peppers. Cook 5 minutes.
3. Add chickpeas and all spices.
4. Toss on high heat for 5 minutes until chickpeas are crispy.
5. Add lemon juice and parsley.
6. Serve with rice, quinoa, or wraps.

Nutrition (per serving): Calories: 310 | Protein: 15g | Carbs: 42g | Fat: 9g | Fiber: 12g

--- RECIPE: Banana Oat Pancakes ---
Category: Breakfast, Sweet, Healthy
Cuisine: Western
Prep Time: 5 minutes
Cook Time: 15 minutes
Total Time: 20 minutes
Servings: 2
Difficulty: Easy
Dietary: Vegetarian, Gluten-Free (use certified GF oats), High-Protein

Ingredients:
- 2 ripe bananas
- 2 eggs
- 1 cup rolled oats
- 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
- 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
- 1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract
- Pinch of salt
- Oil for cooking

Instructions:
1. Blend bananas, eggs, oats, baking powder, cinnamon, vanilla, and salt.
2. Let batter rest 2 minutes.
3. Heat oil in non-stick pan on medium heat.
4. Pour 1/4 cup batter per pancake.
5. Cook until bubbles form on top (3-4 minutes).
6. Flip and cook 2 more minutes.
7. Serve with fresh fruit and honey.

Nutrition (per serving): Calories: 290 | Protein: 12g | Carbs: 48g | Fat: 6g | Fiber: 6g
Tips: Riper bananas make sweeter pancakes. Batter thickens as it sits, add splash of milk if too thick.
